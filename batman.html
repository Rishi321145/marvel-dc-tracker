About

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a billionaire inventor and founding member of the Avengers. Known for his high-tech armored suits and unmatched intelligence, he played a key role in defeating Thanos in Avengers: Endgame.

Powers & Abilities

  • Advanced Iron Suits
  • Artificial Intelligence Integration
  • Genius-level Engineering
  • Energy Repulsors & Flight

  • Avengers Series (2012–2019)
  • Captain America: Civil War
  • Spider-Man: Homecoming
  • Avengers: Endgame (Final appearance)
